Mussels in White Wine Sauce

Home

Date coming up? Cooking dinner for the boss? These fancy Mussels will make sure you succeed at whatever it is you're doing!

Whenever I need a real show stopper for a dinner, I always turn to mussels. Sophisticated, fancy, elegant. Not only are they a great source of protein and other essential nutrients. They also taste great. This dish can server up to 3 to 4 people and goes good with a Greek salad.

Ingredients

  • 4 Ibs of live mussels
  • 6 tablespoons of butter, cut into pieces
  • 4 large shallots, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups dry white wine
  • 1/3 cup of mixed fresh herbs (such as flat-leaf parsley, chervil, or basil, chopped

Instructions

  1. Rinse and scrub mussels under cold water.
  2. Using your fingers or paring knife, remove beards (strings that hang from the mussel shells), and discard. Discard any broken mussels. If any mussels are open and resist being closed, discard them.
  3. In a large stockpot set over medium heat, melt the butter. Once it's hot, add shallots, garlic, and salt.
  4. Add the wine and bring to a boil.
  5. Add mussels and cover
  6. Steam until all mussels are open, about 5 minutes.
  7. Stir in herbs.
  8. Remove from heat.
  9. Divide mussels and broth among four bowls.
  10. Serve immediately
