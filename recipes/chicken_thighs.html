Easy Air Fried Chicken Thighs

Home

Chicken thighs that taste as good as Popeyes without the added calories? Say no more!

These easy Air Fryer Chicken Thighs take only minutes to prep and are very versatile. You can switch up the spices to change the flavors. This recipe can serve 3 to 6 people, depending on how many thighs you want per serving.

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thighs, with bone and skin
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sweet paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried herbs, such as herbs de provence or dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken with the juice of 1/2 of the lemon, then season with remaining spices on both sides.
  2. Rub the seasoning well all over the chicken then transfer to the air fryer, skin side down.
  3. Air fry 400F 12 minutes on each side, until golden and crispy and cooked through in the center.
